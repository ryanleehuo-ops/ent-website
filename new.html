About Dr. Huo

Dr. Jerry Huo, M.D.
Otolaryngology – Head & Neck Surgery

Dr. Jerry Huo is a board-certified Otolaryngologist – Head & Neck Surgeon practicing in Flushing, NY. He received his medical degree from the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and completed his residency in Otolaryngology – Head & Neck Surgery at Manhattan Eye and Ear.

Dr. Huo has been a member of the NewYork-Presbyterian Queens medical staff since 2000, bringing over two decades of experience in the diagnosis and treatment of ear, nose, throat, and head and neck conditions in adult patients.

His practice focuses exclusively on adult patients. Families seeking care for children should contact their pediatrician for a referral to a pediatric ENT specialist.
